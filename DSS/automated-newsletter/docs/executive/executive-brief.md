# DSS Automated Newsletter — Executive Brief

## 1) Purpose & Context
The DSS Automated Newsletter is a content intelligence system designed to
curate, summarize, and distribute company– and industry–focused insights
in a structured and consistent format.

It reduces manual editorial effort and improves executive visibility into
market news, sentiment, and strategic topics.

---

## 2) Core Capabilities
- Automated news collection & filtering
- Company & industry–scoped summarization
- Key takeaway extraction
- Consistent structured output for WhatsApp & internal channels
- Support for multilingual sources (ID / EN)

---

## 3) Primary Users
- Executive stakeholders
- Strategy & planning teams
- Investment & market monitoring teams

---

## 4) Outcomes & Business Value
- Faster information digestion
- Reduced manual curation work
- More consistent reporting format
- Easier knowledge sharing across teams

---

## 5) Current Focus Areas
- Stability & delivery reliability
- Consistent content formatting
- Company–level contextual summaries
- Future integration with sentiment analytics

---

## 6) Roadmap Snapshot
- Short-term: formatting stability, duplicate detection
- Mid-term: per-company thematic streams
- Long-term: automated insight scoring + relevance ranking

## 1) Problem Statement
Manual news curation creates inconsistency in
format, depth, and delivery cadence.

The system automates:
- news collection
- filtering & relevance recognition
- structured summarization
- distribution formatting

---

## 2) Product Goals
- Deliver concise, structured daily briefings
- Maintain consistency across delivery channels
- Support company- and sector-based perspectives

---

## 3) Scope of Coverage
- Corporate news (DSSA + related ecosystem)
- Energy, mining, and technology sectors
- Market & strategic developments

---

## 4) Key Features
- Automated news ingestion
- Relevance classification
- Structured summarization
- Key takeaway extraction
- Duplicate detection
- Delivery formatting engine

---

## 5) Success Metrics
- Delivery success rate
- Duplicate content reduction
- Newsletter reading completion feedback
- Time saved in curation workflow
